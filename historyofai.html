History of AI

AI was first invented in 1956. The first project that used AI was the Dartmouth Project, started by John McCarthy. John McCarthy is now known as the “father of AI”.


Timeline of AI Inventions

1950s

  • Georgetown experiment - 60 Russian sentences translated into English
  • Dartmouth meeting - Term "artificial intelligence" coined

1960s

  • Pattern matching - ELIZA Joseph Weizenbaum, MIT
  • Robinson's complete algorithm for logical reasoning
  • Micro words - SHRDLU, Winograd, MIT

1970s

  • Early development of knowledge based systems

1980s

  • Expert systems industry booms
  • Rapid increase in technical depth of mainstream AI

1990s

  • IBM: The integrated reasoning shell

2000s

  • Resurgence of many AI startups

2010 and Beyond

  • Deep learning and artificial neural networks
  • Image recognition
  • Golden age of AI
