Uses of AI

Branches

AI is a broad field, and saying "I work in AI is" is not specific. A few branches of AI include:

  • Detection, reasoning, and problem solving
  • Perception: Computer vision
  • Knowledge representation
  • Natural language processing
  • Planning
  • Machine learning
  • Social intelligence
  • Robotics
