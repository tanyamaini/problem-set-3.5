    Branches

    AI is a broad field, and saying "I work in AI is" is not specific. A few branches of AI include:

    • Detection, reasoning, and problem solving
    • Perception: Computer vision
    • Knowledge representation
    • Natural language processing
    • Planning
    • Machine learning
    • Social intelligence
    • Robotics

    How to Get Started with AI

    Many universities and colleges in the United States offer a technology major, more specifically something to do with AI. If you want to pursue a career with artifical intelligence, any university with a computer science course will do.
